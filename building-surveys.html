Residential Building Surveys

Residential Building Surveys Purchasing a residential property is one of the most significant financial commitments you will make. Understanding its condition before you proceed is essential to avoiding unexpected costs and making an informed decision. At Polaris, we provide clear, detailed residential building surveys designed to give you a complete understanding of the property’s condition and any associated risks. Our surveys identify defects, highlight areas of concern, and provide practical advice on repairs, maintenance, and likely future expenditure. We present our findings in a clear and accessible format, ensuring you can proceed with confidence—whether that means renegotiating, budgeting for works, or reconsidering the purchase. We tailor our surveys to suit the property type, from traditional buildings to more modern construction, ensuring that the level of detail reflects the complexity of the asset. Our Service Includes • Detailed inspection of the property. • Identification of defects and structural issues. • Advice on necessary repairs and maintenance. • Guidance on future risks and costs.
